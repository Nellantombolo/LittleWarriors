Our Mission & Vision

At Little Warriors Foundation, we are dedicated to supporting children with cancer through emotional, medical, and financial assistance. Our vision is a world where every child fighting cancer gets the support they deserve.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Little Warriors Foundation began as a small community initiative. Today, it has grown into a nationwide network helping families navigate the challenges of childhood cancer with compassion and strength.

Meet the Team

Our team includes doctors, volunteers, cancer survivors, and families — all united by the mission to uplift and empower children fighting cancer.

Core Values

  • Compassion – We care deeply for every child and family.
  • Community – Together, we are stronger.
  • Commitment – We never stop fighting for our cause.
