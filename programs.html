Our Programs

At Little Warriors Foundation, we are committed to standing beside children with cancer and their families through every step of their journey. Our programs are carefully designed to provide emotional support, financial relief, and educational assistance. Whether it's a comforting hospital visit, a much-needed care package, or help with tutoring during treatment, our goal is to bring light and stability during a difficult time. Below is an overview of the core programs we offer and how they make an impact.

Programs
Program Name Description Duration
Hospital Visits Volunteers bring comfort kits, books, and companionship to kids in treatment Weekly
Emergency Fund Helps families pay for travel, medication, or lodging during care Case-by-case
Online Support Groups Virtual meetups for parents or kids to share and connect Bi-weekly
Educational Tutoring Helps kids keep up with schoolwork through virtual or in-person tutoring As scheduled
Care Package Delivery Sends personalized boxes with toys, hygiene items, and notes of encouragement Monthly
